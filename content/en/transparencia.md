In this section, we provide an overview of our financial statements.

Between 2021 and 2025, Political Watch received nearly two million euros, which we detail in [this table](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1SrQ7j6vtcIvOLo3e1jCgfefGRoBQmMhPyETm2ZOO8Rg/edit?usp=sharing).

We present our revenue in a disaggregated format: you can review the funding by source, project name, year of execution, and, of course, the amount received.

Regarding the source of revenue, we distinguish between funds from civil society organizations, the public sector, universities, and private philanthropy.

Regarding the services provided, we also distinguish four categories:
* Technology development
* Training and awareness-raising
* Consulting and research
* Mobilization and advocacy
